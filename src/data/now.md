## Moving to Perth

I'm relocating to Perth, Western Australia, before the end of 2026. Most of my
energy right now goes into the logistics of that move and into mapping the deep
tech landscape on the ground — the research groups, the founders, the capital,
and the industrial problems worth pointing technology at.

## Writing

I'm writing **Ground Truth**, a series of thesis-style essays on frontier
hardware, neural interfaces, and why the next generation of deep tech companies
can be built in Australia. I publish when an argument is actually ready, not on
a schedule.

## What I'm looking for

I'm open to the right investor role or the right founding team in Western
Australian deep tech — energy, minerals, space, and marine systems especially.
If you're working on something hard in that space, I'd like to hear about it.
